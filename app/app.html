Loading gear from the game

1. Install requirements from the instructions
2. Close Epic 7
3. Click Start scanning
4. Open Epic 7, and load into the lobby
5. Click Stop scanning
6. Once the data appears, click Export to save it to a gear.txt
7. Import the gear.txt with the Replace/Add/Merge options below
8. Go to the Gear tab, and use the Level = 0 filter to manually fix any level 0 items











Importing gear data

After the gear set is created, choose one of the following ways to import your gear, then select your "gear.txt" file.
Use replace to completely replace any existing gear with your new gear screenshots. This will wipe existing heroes.
Use add to add your new gear screenshots to your currently loaded gear. Does not wipe heroes.
Use merge to combine your new gear screenshots with your currently loaded gear.
Merging replaces your old items with new items, while keeping your heroes' equipped gear and builds intact.
This is useful for people who re-screenshot all their gear, and want to keep all their hero settings.
